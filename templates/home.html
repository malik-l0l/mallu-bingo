Game Instructions

Bingo is a 2 player game which has so many versions. The instructions for this version are given below:

  • Create a board with 5 rows and 5 columns which have numbers from 1-25 placed randomly.

    Note:
    Each player must hide their board throughout the game until finish.
    Player1's board may or may not be the same as player2's board.

  • Player1 will tell a number which is not marked in the board, then both players mark that number in their board.

  • Player2 will tell a number which is not marked in the board, then both players mark that number in their board.

  • Repeat steps 2 and 3 until someone wins.

  • If every number in a row, column, or diagonal is marked, the player gets a point which is represented as 'B I N G O'.

    Example:
Given board =
[
    ["█", "█", "█", "█", "█"],
    [13 , 15 , 20 , "█", "█" ],
    [11 , 12 , "█", 14 , "█"],
    [16 , "█", 18 , 19 , "█"],
    ["█", 22 , 23 , 24 , "█"],
]
Here, the player with this board gets 3 points (1 row, 1 col, 1 diagonal)
which is represented as 'B I N'.

  • Whoever gets 5 points AKA 'B I N G O' first WINS.
